About Bulbau

A Luxembourg-based engineering company specialising in energy and building services since 2015.

Our Story

Engineering for a sustainable future

Bulbau was founded in Luxembourg in 2015 with a clear mission: to bring precision engineering and honest craftsmanship to the energy and building services sector. From our first residential solar installation to large-scale commercial projects, we have built a reputation for on-time delivery, transparent pricing, and work that lasts.

Our team of certified engineers and technicians covers the full spectrum of building energy systems — photovoltaics, electrical infrastructure, energy storage, building automation, industrial lighting, and heat pumps. Every project begins with a detailed site survey and energy audit, so our recommendations are always grounded in real data.

We operate across all three regions of Luxembourg, working with private homeowners, architects, property developers, and industrial clients. Whether the brief is a single rooftop solar array or a complete building systems overhaul, we apply the same rigour: measure twice, install once.

Bulbau holds all required certifications under Luxembourg law and is a registered member of the Fédération des Artisans. We carry full professional indemnity and public liability insurance on every project.
