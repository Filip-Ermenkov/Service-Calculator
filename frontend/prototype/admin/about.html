About Us Page — Main Content
This text appears in the "Our Story" section of the About Us page. Write in English — translations are generated automatically.

Bulbau was founded in Luxembourg in 2015 with a clear mission: to bring precision engineering and honest craftsmanship to the energy and building services sector. From our first residential solar installation to large-scale commercial projects, we have built a reputation for on-time delivery, transparent pricing, and work that lasts.


Our team of certified engineers and technicians covers the full spectrum of building energy systems — photovoltaics, electrical infrastructure, energy storage, building automation, industrial lighting, and heat pumps.
